The design of this visualization is largely inspired by what I saw during my critique assignment, when I analyzed what I thought worked and what I thought didn't.

To begin, a simple bar chart was chosen for this visualization because the comparison that is being made is relatively simple. The dataset provides a snapshot of data, without dates for the sake of comparisons over time.
